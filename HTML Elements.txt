	1. Paragraphs
		a. <p> and </p>
		b. Use spaces above and below the start of a new paragraph to separate on web page
		c. If no tags, text will be continuous
	2. <br>
		a. Creates a line break in a paragraph
		b. Can add <br> within a paragraph to separate lines
	3. Whitespace
		a. Multiple spaces will be condensed to one space
		b. Ex
			i. If    I     put too many    spaces
				1) Will appear as If I put too many spaces
	4. Sections and Headings
		a. <section>
			i. Related content with a section element
		b. Heading
			i. <h1>, through <h6>
				1) H1 for top level titles
				2) H2 for sub levels
				3) So on
			ii. Should be outside a <p> element and immediately before
	5. More text formatting
		a. Em - italic
		b. Strong - bold
		c. Cite - denotes a title, italicized by default
		d. Mark - highlighter
		e. B - bold
		f. I - alternate language/meaning - italics
		g. U - underlined
		h. Q - quote
